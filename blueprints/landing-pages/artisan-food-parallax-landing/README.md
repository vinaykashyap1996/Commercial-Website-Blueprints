## What You'll Build

A premium artisan food brand landing page featuring **multi-layer parallax scrolling** with floating ingredients, product photography, and rich textures that move at different speeds as the user scrolls. The page includes a full-viewport parallax hero, a brand origin story section, a product showcase grid with hover effects, a "bean to bar" process timeline, customer testimonials, and a newsletter signup — all tied together with smooth scroll-triggered reveal animations.

This is the kind of elegant, scroll-driven experience that artisan food brands, specialty coffee roasters, craft breweries, and premium restaurant groups pay $3,000-$8,000 for.

## What You'll Learn

- **Multi-layer parallax scrolling** — 4 layers moving at different speeds using GSAP ScrollTrigger
- **Scroll-triggered reveal animations** — staggered entrance effects on sections and grids
- **GSAP ScrollTrigger** integration for scroll-linked animation control
- **Lenis smooth scroll** for a buttery scrolling experience
- **CSS design system** with custom properties, warm color palette, and serif/sans-serif type pairing
- **Responsive design** with 3 breakpoints (1024px, 768px, 480px)
- **Product card hover effects** — zoom, overlay, and lift animations
- **Sticky navigation** with scroll-based style transitions (transparent → solid)
